Ayrton Senna da Silva

The greatest F1 racer of all time

Here's a timeline of Ayrton Senna's Life:

  • 1960 - Born in Sao Paulo, Brazil
  • 1977 - Won the South American Kart Championship
  • 1978 - Entered the Karting World Championship
  • 1982 - Offered to drive for a Formula Ford 2000 team for 10,000 GBP
  • 1983 - Entered Formula Three, racing for the West Surrey Racing team
  • 1984 - Joins Toleman for the 1984 Formula One season
  • 1985 - Joins Lotus for the 1985 Formula One season
  • 1985 - Took his first pole position and Grand Prix victory at the Portuguese Grand Prix
  • 1988 - Joins McLaren with Alain Prost as a teammate
  • 1988 - Wins his First Formula One Championship with McLaren
  • 1990 - Wins his Second Formula One Championship with McLaren
  • 1991 - Wins the Brazilian Grand Prix for the first time in his hometown of Sao Paulo
  • 1991 - Wins his Third Formula One Championship with McLaren
  • 1994 - Joins Williams for the 1994 Formula One Season
  • 1994 - Died at the age of 34, due to a crash during the 1994 San Marino Grand Prix
  • 1994 - After his death, it was discovered that he had donated 400 million US dollars worth of his fortune in secret

“Senna was the best driver who ever lived”
